Our Business Models

Flexible solutions tailored to meet diverse transportation needs

In-House Driver Model

This model utilizes exclusively employed drivers who undergo rigorous training programs and adhere to strict company standards. Benefits include:

  • Consistent service quality across all rides
  • Dedicated support team for driver management
  • Enhanced safety protocols and regular vehicle inspections
  • Comprehensive insurance coverage for passengers

Hybrid Mix Model

A balanced approach combining in-house drivers with carefully vetted third-party partners. Key advantages include:

  • Scalability during peak demand periods
  • Expanded geographic coverage without compromising quality
  • Cost optimization while maintaining service standards
  • Flexibility to adapt to market conditions
